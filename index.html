Street Fighter 6

You will be given a random nummber at the start of the game.

There are four fighters below. By clicking on a fighter you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each fighter is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts the value of the fighters will change.
