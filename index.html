Street Fighter 6

1: You will be given a random nummber at the start of the game.

2: There are four fighters below. By clicking on a fighter you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3: You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4: The value of each fighter is hidden from you until you click on it.

5: Each time the game restarts the value of the fighters will change.
